IIT Kanpur Today!

As we all know, guided by the motto ‘Tamaso mā jyotirgamaya’ (Lead me from darkness to light), IIT Kanpur continues to nurture bright minds with intellect, courage, and integrity to address the biggest challenges faced by humankind. Renowned for its excellence in science and engineering education, IIT Kanpur has made significant contributions to research and development over the decades. Today the campus spans across 1,050 acres of lush green flora hosting a rich array of academic and research resources. The institute comprises 19 departments, three interdisciplinary programs, 26 centers, and three specialized schools across engineering, science, design, humanities, and management disciplines. With over 570 full-time faculty and more than 9,500 students, IIT Kanpur continues to be a leader in fostering innovation and academic rigor.

About FACE 2025

The Faculty Alumni Collaborative Endeavour (FACE) 2025, organized by IIT Kanpur, is set to take place on October 3, 2025 at Stanford University. This inaugural global symposium will bring together renowned IITK alumni, thought leaders, researchers, and academic pioneers from around the world.

Designed as a platform to foster long-term academic collaboration, the event will spotlight strategic opportunities in higher education, research, and innovation. FACE 2025 aims to further strengthen IIT Kanpur’s engagement with the global academic and research community.

Collaborative Purpose

The symposium will serve as a platform to strengthen IIT Kanpur’s academic engagement with the global research ecosystem while nurturing long-term institutional partnerships.
