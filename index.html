Designed as a platform to foster long-term academic collaboration, the event will spotlight strategic opportunities in higher education, research, and innovation. FACE 2025 aims to further strengthen IIT Kanpur‚Äôs engagement with the global academic and research community.

The Faculty Alumni Collaborative Endeavour (FACE) aims to bring together faculty from IIT Kanpur, alumni who are now in academic positions globally, as well as leading industrial researchers, to deliberate on emerging areas of research and catalyze collaborative academic programs.

The symposium seeks to foster deeper engagement between IIT Kanpur and the global academic and research community, promote meaningful partnerships, and build a strong network of scholars. It will also serve as an opportunity to connect with alumni and early-career researchers who are interested in exploring faculty roles at IIT Kanpur.
