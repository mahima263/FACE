About FACE 2025

The Faculty Alumni Collaborative Endeavour (FACE) 2025, organized by IIT Kanpur, is set to take place on October 3, 2025, at Stanford University. This inaugural global symposium will bring together renowned IITK alumni, thought leaders, researchers, and academic pioneers from around the world.

Designed as a platform to foster long-term academic collaboration, the event will spotlight strategic opportunities in higher education, research, and innovation. Through visionary discussions and shared insights, FACE 2025 aims to shape the future of interdisciplinary scholarship and strengthen IIT Kanpur‚Äôs engagement with the global academic and research community.
